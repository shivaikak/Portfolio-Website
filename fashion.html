Fashion and Art

Celebrating creativity, expression, and the intersection of fashion, art, and technology.

My Journey in Fashion

My love for fashion design and art started at just 3 years old. When I was 15, I got my first sewing machine and began designing my own clothes, altering my closet to create unique pieces that reflected my style.

Business Savvy

I run a Depop shop where I sell my old clothes, demonstrating my entrepreneurial spirit and business management skills. Managing this shop has honed my ability to handle marketing, customer relations, and logistics.

The Future of Fashion and Technology

I am fascinated by the intersection of technology and fashion and look forward to exploring how innovations like wearable tech and AI-driven designs will shape the industry. One day, I hope to have a side business where I design and sell one-of-a-kind pieces, creating a fashion line that reflects my vision and creativity.

The Importance of Arts and Aesthetics

Art is a way for me to express myself and romanticize the world we live in. I love how aspects of computer science welcome creativity, whether it's through designing products or finding innovative solutions to problems. Arts and aesthetics will always hold a special place in my heart as a means of personal expression.
